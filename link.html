ABOUT THE COURSE

MCA, also known as the Master of Computer Application is a postgraduate program in computer application and sciences. The duration of this course is two years. The program is specifically designed for students who have a solid theoretical knowledge of computers and other operating software. This course is intended to offer industry-oriented modern education in applied computer science. Currently, the IT industry is booming with the demand for fresh MCA candidates, and going for this course would be a great opportunity for anyone who is seeking a high-salaried job in the same field. Before you take the plunge of filling out forms of your preferred institute, check out the course eligibility, fees, career, jobs, scope, syllabus, and other details of the MCA course.
